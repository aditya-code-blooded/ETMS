Welcome to Expenses and Todo Management System



Expenses and Todo Management System (ETMS) allows you to keep track of your expenses and maintain your to-do list.


Navigate to the 'Expenses' link above and you can see your list of expenses. You can add/ remove the expense reports with ease. Want to visualize how you are spending your money? Don't worry! Click on 'generate graph' to generate a graphical representation of your expenses over a period of time.


You can also manage your to-do lists so that you are up-to-date with your work. Simply navigate to 'To do' link above where you can add/remove your to-do items.


Last but not the least to view your profile information click on 'My Profile' link. Here you can update your profile information and view some interesting statistics. You can logout and return back to us later whenever you want by clicking on 'logout' button at the top.
